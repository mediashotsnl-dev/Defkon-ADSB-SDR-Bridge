# Privacy

DEFKON ADSB SDR BRIDGE receives local USB radio samples, decodes ADS-B traffic,
and serves the result only over localhost to compatible client applications. It
does not contain analytics, advertising, account login, or developer telemetry
and does not send decoded traffic or personal data to MediaShots NL.

Diagnostic and crash information shown by the Bridge is stored locally on the
Android device. The optional fallback-driver and source-code buttons open
separate applications or websites selected by the user; their own privacy terms
then apply.
